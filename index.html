My Favorite Films

I have seen many films these past couple of years, and I've experienced stories that I will always remember, but there are five that have impacted not only my view of film but also of life.

Honorable Mentions

  • A Seperation by Asghar Farhadi (Iran)
  • Stalker by Andrei Tarkovsky (USSR)
  • A Scene at the Sea by Takeshi Kitano (Japan)
  • 12 Angry Men by Sidney Lumet (USA)
  • Mishima: A Life in Four Chapters by Paul Schrader (Japan, USA)

My Top 5 Films

  1. Spirited Away by Hayao Miyazaki (Japan)
  2. Eternity and a Day by Theo Angelopoulos (Greece)
  3. Parasite by Bong Joon-ho (South Korea)
  4. La Haine by Mathieu Kassovitz (France)
  5. In the Mood for Love by Wong Kar-Wai (Hong Kong)
