Eternity and a Day

Why I Love It

When I first introduced this film, I completely related to the main character in multiple ways; his name was Alexandros (mine is Alexander), he was a writer and a hermit. I felt drawn to him on a personal level but was also deeply moved by the atmosphere of the film.

Highlights

  • Achieved a Potent Sense of Magical Realism
