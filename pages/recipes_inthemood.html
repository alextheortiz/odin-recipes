In the Mood for Love

Why I Love It

Heralded by many as one of the greatest films of the century so far, In the Mood for Love changed how I viewed storytelling in a fundamental way. Wong Kar-Wai's expert handle of time and place transformed my viewing experience from what could've been a simple, passive form of entertainment to an examination of love, betrayal, regret, doubt, and everything else in between; such a nuanced view of human nature that I cannot stop showering with praise.
